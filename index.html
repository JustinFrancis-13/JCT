About Joseph Charitable Trust

Our Story

Joseph Paul Charitable Trust was founded in 2025 by Pastor Kandi Anil Reddy and Pastor Rohini Reddy, the loving parents of Brother Kandi Joseph Paul Reddy. Guided by God’s love and blessings, they started the trust to help people in need and bring hope into the lives of the poor, widows, children, and elderly. The Trust began by providing meals to the hungry and ration to widows, and has grown into a mission to support communities through love, care, and service. Every action is inspired by faith, aiming to reflect God’s compassion and make a positive difference in people’s lives. The founders believe that helping others is a way to serve God, and through the Trust, they hope to bring relief, joy, and opportunity to those who need it most. In the future, the Trust plans to support education, build orphanages, and create old-age homes, spreading God’s love through every initiative.

Our Mission

To serve the underprivileged with love, care, and compassion, guided by God’s grace, by providing food, essential supplies, education, and support to widows, children, the elderly, and vulnerable communities. The Trust is committed to creating positive and lasting change in people’s lives through selfless service.

Our Vision

To build a world where every child has access to education, every elderly person lives with dignity and care, and no family suffers from hunger or neglect. Inspired by God’s love, the Trust aims to establish orphanages, old-age homes, and educational programs, bringing hope, joy, and opportunity to those in need.
