About Joseph Charitable Trust

Our Story

Founded in 2005, Joseph Charitable Trust began as a small community initiative in response to the devastating effects of natural disasters in our region. What started as a temporary relief effort has grown into a comprehensive charity organization serving thousands of people annually.

Our journey began when our founders, Dr. Shiny Reddy and Dr. Ravali, witnessed the plight of families affected by floods and decided to take action. They mobilized local resources and volunteers to provide immediate assistance, and soon realized the need for sustained support in these communities.

Today, we operate in over 50 communities across three states, with a dedicated team of staff and volunteers working tirelessly to bring positive change to those who need it most.

Our Mission

To alleviate suffering and empower underprivileged communities by providing essential services, education, and opportunities for sustainable development.

Our Vision

A world where every individual has access to basic necessities, quality education, healthcare, and the opportunity to reach their full potential.
